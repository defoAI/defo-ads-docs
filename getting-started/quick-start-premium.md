# Quick Start — Premium

Defo Ads Premium gives you cloud sync, managed AI, Google Ads integration, and team collaboration. Here's how to get started.

---

## What You Get

- Everything in the free version, plus:
- **Cloud sync** — Access your campaigns from any device
- **Managed AI** — No API key needed, we handle the AI infrastructure
- **Google Ads integration** — Import and export directly to/from Google Ads
- **Performance dashboard** — Real-time analytics and KPIs
- **AI Assistant** — Chat-based campaign management with natural language
- **Asset library** — Upload and manage campaign images
- **Team collaboration** — Invite members and share campaigns
- **Free trial** — Try managed AI features before subscribing

---

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit Defo Ads and click **"Sign Up"** or **"Upgrade to Premium"**
2. Create an account with email/password or sign in with Google
3. You'll automatically get a **free trial** of managed AI features

![Sign up](../images/premium-signup.png)

---

## Step 2: Choose a Plan

1. Go to the **Premium** page (or you'll be prompted after signup)
2. Compare available plans — each includes different AI quotas and campaign limits
3. Select a plan and complete payment via Stripe
4. Your subscription activates immediately

> **Tip:** Not ready to pay? Your free trial gives you access to managed AI for a limited time. You can also use your own OpenAI API key at any time.

See [Subscription & Billing](../premium/subscription.md) for plan details.

---

## Step 3: Connect Google Ads

1. Go to **Integrations** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Connect"** next to Google Ads
3. Sign in with your Google account and authorize access
4. Select which Google Ads accounts to connect (supports multiple accounts and MCC)

![Connect Google Ads](../images/connect-google-ads.png)

See [Google Ads Connection](../premium/google-ads-connection.md) for detailed setup.

---

## Step 4: Import Existing Campaigns

If you already have campaigns in Google Ads:

1. Go to **Sync** in the sidebar
2. Select **Import** direction
3. Choose which campaigns to import from your Google Ads account
4. Click **"Start Sync"** and watch the real-time progress

![Sync import](../images/sync-import.png)

Your campaigns, ad groups, keywords, and ads will be imported into Defo Ads for editing.

---

## Step 5: Create or Edit Campaigns

You can now:
- **Edit imported campaigns** — Optimize ad copy, keywords, and settings
- **Create new campaigns** — Use the AI-powered wizard (same as free version, but with managed AI)
- **Use the AI Assistant** — Click the floating button (bottom-right) and type natural language requests like:
  - *"Create a search campaign for summer shoes with $50/day budget"*
  - *"Add keywords about running shoes to my fitness campaign"*

---

## Step 6: Sync Back to Google Ads

When your campaigns are ready:

1. Go to **Sync** in the sidebar
2. Select campaigns to export
3. Assign Google Ads accounts to unlinked campaigns
4. Click **"Start Sync"** to push changes live
5. Monitor real-time progress with per-entity status updates

---

## Your Premium Dashboard

Once connected, your dashboard shows:

- **Performance KPIs** — Spend, clicks, impressions, CTR, conversions
- **Trend charts** — Interactive graphs with period comparison
- **Top campaigns** — Ranked by performance with sparkline trends
- **Activity feed** — Recent imports, exports, syncs, and changes

See [Performance Dashboard](../premium/performance-dashboard.md) for details.
