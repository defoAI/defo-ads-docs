# Quick Start — Premium

Defo Ads Premium gives you cloud sync, managed AI, multi-platform integration, and team collaboration. Here's how to get started.

---

## What You Get

- Everything in the free version, plus:
- **Cloud sync** — Access your campaigns from any device
- **Managed AI** — No API key needed, we handle the AI infrastructure
- **Multi-platform integration** — Connect Google Ads and Microsoft Advertising
- **Performance dashboard** — Real-time analytics and KPIs
- **AI Assistant** — Chat-based campaign management with natural language
- **Asset library** — Upload and manage campaign images
- **Team collaboration** — Invite members and share campaigns
- **Conversion tracking** — Create and manage conversion actions across platforms
- **Free trial** — Try managed AI features before subscribing

---

## Step 1: Onboarding Wizard

When you first visit Defo Ads, the **onboarding wizard** guides you through setup in 6 steps:

1. **Welcome** — Choose your language (English, Deutsch, or Français) and click **Get Started**
2. **Account** — Sign in with Google (or accept terms if returning). No password needed.
3. **About You** — Optionally enter your organization name and select what you want to use Defo Ads for
4. **Choose a Plan** — Select a plan (Free Trial, Pro, or Business). Free/trial plans are claimed instantly; paid plans redirect to Stripe checkout.
5. **Connect Platforms** — Optionally connect Google Ads and/or Microsoft Advertising via OAuth
6. **Done** — Review your setup summary and choose what to do first

## Step 2: Connect Your Platforms

If you didn't connect during onboarding:

1. Go to **Connections** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Connect"** next to Google Ads or Microsoft Advertising
3. Sign in with your platform account and authorize access
4. Select which accounts to connect (supports multiple accounts and MCC for Google Ads)

![Connect Google Ads](../images/connect-google-ads.png)

See [Connections](../premium/integrations.md) for detailed setup.

---

## Step 3: Import Existing Campaigns

If you already have campaigns on your ad platforms:

1. Go to **Sync** in the sidebar
2. Your existing campaigns appear in the **Pull** section
3. Select which campaigns to import
4. Click **"Sync"** and watch the real-time progress
5. When complete, a **Sync Record** summary shows what was imported

![Sync import](../images/sync-import.png)

Your campaigns, ad groups, keywords, and ads will be imported into Defo Ads for editing. Site statistics (sites created, reused, skipped) are displayed in the import summary.

---

## Step 4: Create or Edit Campaigns

You can now:
- **Edit imported campaigns** — Optimize ad copy, keywords, and settings
- **Create new campaigns** — Use the AI-powered wizard (with managed AI — no API key needed)
- **Use the AI Assistant** — Click the floating button (bottom-right) and type natural language requests like:
  - *"Create a search campaign for summer shoes with $50/day budget"*
  - *"Add keywords about running shoes to my fitness campaign"*
- **Watch for AI suggestion chips** — Context-aware action suggestions appear below the chat

---

## Step 5: Sync Back to Your Platforms

When your campaigns are ready:

1. Go to **Sync** in the sidebar
2. The **Push** section shows campaigns ready to send to your platforms
3. Toggle which platforms each campaign should be pushed to
4. Click **"Sync"** to push changes live
5. Monitor real-time progress with per-entity status updates
6. View the **Sync Record** summary when complete

> **Note:** If you delete a campaign that's linked to a platform, it appears in the **Remove** section. Deletions are deferred until you sync, giving you a chance to restore.

## Your Premium Dashboard

Once connected, your dashboard shows:

- **Plan status indicator** in the top nav — shows your current plan, trial days remaining, or renewal date
- **Performance KPIs** — Spend, clicks, impressions, CTR, conversions
- **Trend charts** — Interactive graphs with period comparison
- **Top campaigns** — Ranked by performance with sparkline trends
- **Activity feed** — Recent syncs, changes, and AI insights

See [Performance Dashboard](../premium/performance-dashboard.md) for details.
