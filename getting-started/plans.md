## Free Trial

Every new account includes a free trial with access to all features at reduced quotas. No credit card required.

- **Duration:** Configured per deployment (typically 14 days)
- **Full feature access:** All features available with trial-level quotas
- **No commitment:** Explore everything before subscribing

---

## Paid Plans

Paid plans scale by usage limits and feature access. Higher tiers typically include:

- Higher daily AI token and image generation limits
- More platform accounts (Google Ads, Microsoft Ads)
- Access to more capable AI models
- Team collaboration features
- More scheduler slots for automated syncs

Plans and pricing are configured dynamically and displayed on the plan selection page in the app.

---

## Optional: Bring Your Own API Key

All plans include managed AI with no setup required. If you prefer, you can also use your own OpenAI API key — configure this in **Settings > General**. Your key is stored locally in your browser and AI requests go directly to OpenAI.
