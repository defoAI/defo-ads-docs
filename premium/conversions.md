# Conversions

Track and manage conversion actions across your connected advertising platforms. The **Conversions** page lets you create conversion actions, sync them with your ad platforms, and get tracking code snippets for your website.

---

## Accessing Conversions

Click **Conversions** in the sidebar (under the **Platforms** section). This opens the Conversions management page.

![Conversions page](../images/conversions-overview.png)

---

## What Are Conversion Actions?

Conversion actions track valuable activities that users take after clicking your ads, such as:

- **Purchases** — Completed transactions on your website
- **Sign-ups** — Newsletter subscriptions or account registrations
- **Phone calls** — Calls made from your ads or website
- **Form submissions** — Contact forms, quote requests, etc.
- **Page views** — Visits to specific pages (e.g., a "Thank You" page)

Tracking conversions helps you understand which campaigns, ads, and keywords drive the most valuable results.

---

## Creating a Conversion Action

1. Click **Create Conversion Action** at the top of the page.
2. Fill in the conversion action details in the wizard:
   - **Name** — A descriptive name (e.g., "Purchase", "Newsletter Signup")
   - **Category** — The type of conversion (e.g., Purchase, Lead, Sign-up)
   - **Platform** — Which ad platform this conversion applies to
3. Click **Save** to create the action.

---

## Syncing Conversions from Platforms

If you already have conversion actions set up in Google Ads or Microsoft Advertising:

1. Click **Sync from Platform** at the top of the page.
2. Defo Ads will retrieve your existing conversion actions from connected platforms.
3. The actions appear in the table, ready for management.

---

## Managing Conversion Actions

The conversions table shows all your conversion actions with these columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The conversion action name |
| **Platform** | Which ad platform it belongs to (Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising) |
| **Type** | The conversion type (e.g., Website, Phone Call) |
| **Category** | The conversion category (e.g., Purchase, Lead) |
| **Status** | Active or Paused |

### Filtering

Use the filters at the top to narrow the list:

- **Platform** — Show conversions for a specific platform
- **Status** — Filter by Active or Paused status

### Actions

For each conversion action, you can:

- **Get Tracking Code** — Copy the tracking code snippet to add to your website
- **Pause/Resume** — Toggle the conversion action status
- **Delete** — Remove the conversion action (Google Ads only)

---

## Tracking Code

To track conversions on your website, you need to add a tracking code snippet:

1. Find the conversion action in the table.
2. Click the **code icon** to open the Tracking Code dialog.
3. Copy the provided code snippet.
4. Add it to the relevant page on your website (e.g., your order confirmation page).

---

## Empty States

- **No platforms connected** — You'll see a prompt to connect a platform first. Click the link to go to the [Ad Platforms](integrations.md) page.
- **No conversion actions** — Click **Create Conversion Action** to set up your first one.
